Ivy Extract comes from the leaves of the Hedera helix plant and is well-known for its health benefits, especially for the respiratory system. Glucoraphanin is a natural compound found in vegetables like broccoli. It turns into sulforaphane, a powerful antioxidant that helps detoxify the body and protect it from long-term health issues.
